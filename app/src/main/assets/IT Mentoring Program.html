IT Mentoring Program

The program is one of the most important needs this time, whatever the field in which you are working. AAPG, in cooperation with WHIPIX, provided an IT Mentoring Program, through which you could have a base and knowledge of programming logic that could help you in your field after that.

The course included Website Design Diploma, Hecon Online and Offline.It lasted for two months; during each month there was a 3 days Camp and the rest of the month the course was Online, and at the end of the course there was a Project.

The selection was moderated by Interviews that were held on Sunday 19/2.

Date : 18 Feb, 2017

Location : Suez , Egypt
