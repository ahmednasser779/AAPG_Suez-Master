Kuwait Energy Drilling & Production Sessions

To be an accomplished petroleum engineer, you must understand the principles and latest technology of oil well drilling and production engineering.
As we believe that real learning is gained by sharing knowledge. So AAPG in collaboration with Kuwait Energy Company provided two day technical sessions. The First day was about Drilling Engineering and the Second was in Production Engineering.

Date : Wednesday & Thursday, 1st & 2nd February 2017.

Location : KEE Company Headquarters, Cairo

Drilling day included:

  • Well Control
  • Drilling Technology

Production day included:

  • Well Completion
  • Artificial Lift
